This is your first post.

From here, you can write plain Markdown files inside `_posts/` and GitHub Pages will turn them into blog pages automatically.

Things this setup already gives you:

- a homepage
- a blog index
- individual post pages
- an apps section for little tools and experiments

As you grow the site, you can keep the design minimal or gradually add more sections.
